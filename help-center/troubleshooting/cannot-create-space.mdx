description: Solution for space creation errors.

## Issue

- "Create Space" button is disabled
- Error message: "Unable to create space"

## Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Permissions">
    Make sure you are logged in with an account that has space creation rights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the Page">
    Try refreshing your dashboard and clearing your browser cache.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If the issue persists, email support@self-comp.com with a screenshot.
  </Step>
</Steps>
