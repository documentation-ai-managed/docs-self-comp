---
title: Adding a Team Member
description: Invite teammates to your Self-comp space.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Space">
    Select your documentation space from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Members Tab">
    Click the "Members" section in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Member">
    Enter the user's email and click "Invite". They will receive an email to join.
  </Step>
</Steps>
